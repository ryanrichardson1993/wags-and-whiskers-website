About Us and our Values

At Wags & Whiskers we provide animal services including training, dog walking and pet sitting in Hull and surrounding areas.

Bespoke, Stimulating Solo Walks

We walk dogs on a solo basis unless you request otherwise. For us it is important to give your dog our full focus.

We don't just take your dog to an empty field and leave them to run around. We take them on a mentally stimulating walk where they'll have chance to sniff, dig and explore. They'll be mentally enriched as well as physically exercised.

Positive and Responsible Training

We use aversive-free training methods. We don't believe there is ever a good reason to use punishment or force to change a dog's behaviour.
