Our Team

Steph

Steph is the owner of Wags & Whiskers. She specialises in dog training and has a degree in Animal Training and Behaviour. Steph has previously worked in doggy daycare and canine hydrotherapy.

She has grown up around dogs and first trained her family's dog when she was 7 years old. She also loves rabbits and has two lion-lop house bunnies.

Jemma

Jemma provides overnight dog sitting and home pet visits for Wags & Whiskers. She has experience of working with all kinds of dog breeds. Jemma has previously worked in a rescue and rehoming centre, where she gained experience of working with dogs of all sizes and temperaments.

Jemma has two dogs and a whole zoo of other rescue animals at home.

Carol

Carol provides dog walking and pet visits in the West Hull villages. She has lots of experience caring for dogs and cats built up over many years.

She has a pug and a greyhound in the family and likes to spend weekends walking in the country with them.

Abbie

Abbie does dog walking and pet visits, initially joining the team as maternity cover to ensure that our existing dogs still get their walks. She has a degree in Canine and Feline Behaviour from Askham Bryan College.

Abbie has loved dogs since she was a young child and now has a rescue dog of her own called Lassie.
