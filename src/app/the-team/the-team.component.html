Our Team

Steph

Steph is the owner of Wags & Whiskers. She specialises in dog training and has a degree in Animal Training and Behaviour. Steph has previously worked in doggy daycare and canine hydrotherapy.

She has grown up around dogs and first trained her family's dog when she was 7 years old. She also really loves rabbits and has two lion-lop house bunnies.

Jemma

Jemma provides overnight dog sitting and home pet visits for Wags & Whiskers. She has experience of working with all kinds of dog breeds. Jemma has previously worked in a rescue and rehoming centre, where she gained experience of working with dogs of all sizes and temperaments.

Jemma has two jack russells and two rabbits, all of which were rescue animals.
